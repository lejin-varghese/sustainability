Why was the DKSH Identity changed?

As our “old” vision of being the leading Market Expansion Services provider in Asia was largely implemented, it was therefore time to redefine our DKSH Identity and values for the company we are today.

Our new Identity better presents our ambition for future growth and explains our purpose and the value we bring to stakeholders in a clear and crisp way.

What has changed?
  • We introduced a purpose to help us communicate our reason for being
  • We revamped our vision to better define the business partner we want to be
  • Our strategy basically remains the same but has been complemented by additional information on how we want to achieve our long-term goals
  • We revisited the values to better express the standards we adhere to and to guide our decisions and culture ensuring our future success
  • We updated our promise to more clearly articulate the value that we deliver to all our partners
Has our tagline changed?
  • Yes, the updated promise is reflected in our new tagline, which is “Delivering growth – in Asia and beyond.” We will no longer use our old tagline “Think Asia. Think DKSH.”
  • We have updated all Office templates to include the new tagline. You can find them in the Group Marketing Toolbox
Do I need to throw away all old material that still shows the old tagline “Think Asia. Think DKSH.”?

No, you can still use the existing material. If you produce any new materials, please ensure it has the new tagline “Delivering growth – in Asia and beyond.” on it. If you have any questions, reach out to your local Brand Champion.

Do I need to change my email signature?

Yes, you should change your email signature to show the new tagline. You will find a simple template and how-to guide here. You can also reach out to your local Brand Champion with any questions you have.

How was the new Identity developed?

A first proposal was put together based on interviews with a large number of leaders across BUs, Functions and regions as well as a review of existing models and materials.

A second proposal was developed through workshops and meetings with the CEO and put to the Annual Managers Meeting (AMM) and Executive Committee for feedback.

An external agency then validated and refined the proposal and the Identity was signed off by the DKSH Board of Directors in December 2019.

When and how will the Identity be implemented?

The Identity was launched on February 10, 2020, and the individual elements will be gradually introduced to employees during February, March and April and at an Identity Experience Day in April.

Materials will be updated in a step-wise process throughout 2020. In the long-term, the Identity will be integrated into relevant HR and other internal processes and programs in a meaningful way.

What does “and beyond” mean in the promise?

“And beyond” reflects all markets outside of Asia that we are currently also operating in.

Will the Employer Value Proposition and Leadership Principles change?

For now, we do not plan to change the Employer Value Proposition and Leadership Principles.
