A new Identity for DKSH!

Our new DKSH Identity reflects what we stand for and aim for as a company in a crisp and clear way.

Why do we need a new Identity? Over the past 150 years, our company has developed from a traditional trading house to the leading Market Expansion Services provider with a focus on Asia. As our “old” vision was largely implemented, it was therefore time to redefine our DKSH Identity and values for the company we are today.

Why is the Identity important for you? The DKSH Identity and values provide us all with a common guide, bringing us together as one strong DKSH team working towards a shared goal. This will enable us to continue on our "ever better" journey as well as to keep you, and your development, at the heart of our culture.

Our purpose

Enriching people’s lives.

We provide access to high quality products, services and insights creating sustainable value for our partners and generating jobs.

Vision, strategy, promise

Being the trusted partner.

We are the trusted partner for companies looking to grow their business in Asia and beyond.

Our strategy

Growing our four Business Units, strengthening our service offering and increasing operational efficiency.

We are pursuing our strategy by implementing an agile organization, embracing digital technologies to adapt to ever-changing market environments and ensuring sustainable, profitable growth.

Our values

Integrity

We never compromise on our ethical, business and compliance standards.

Empowerment

We own our decisions, take accountability for our actions and trust and respect each other – so each one of us can make a difference and grow.

Collaboration

We work as one DKSH team, supporting each other and our partners, to achieve joint success.

Entrepreneurship

We innovate, identify business opportunities and focus on operational excellence to drive growth for our partners and us.

Sustainability

We take responsibility for our environmental, social and economic impact to account for present and future needs.

Our promise

Delivering growth – in Asia and beyond.
